Why Paktor?

Paktor was founded in July 2013 with the mission of connecting people in Asia through technology-enabled platforms.

With one of the largest user bases in the region of over 5 million, Paktor has facilitated 5 billion profile reviews to date.

Leveraging Paktor’ s carefully crafted proprietary technology and first-mover advantage in the region, Paktor’ s flagship app has become the most successful social networking app for singles in Southeast Asia and Taiwan.

Anonymity

Paktor works on a double blind concept.Only if two parties like each other will we then make an introduction. When you like someone, the person will not know until he/she likes you back. This removes any tension caused by "liking" someone for fear of rejection. We take your privacy very seriously and do not share anything without your consent.

Locality

Paktor is a network of interesting people around you. We think that's where all the fun happens.

Usability

Paktor is easy and fun to use. Our primary focus is to make finding a date simple and fuss-free. Download, sign-up and you're ready to go in less than 30 seconds. No need to spend hours crafting a profile to encompass who you are as a person!
