What is Paktor Labs?

Paktor Labs is the division responsible for developing, acquiring and accelerating nascent and early-stage social apps. It is an experimental and high-paced lab within the Paktor Group where projects experience guided growth, prove themselves, and become the “next big thing.”


The current Paktor Labs projects include Down (and its sister-app, Sweet), Taiwan’s Goodnight, Brazil-based Kickoff, as well as additional projects still in beta. Paktor Labs is staffed by a team of 14 and has a combined user base of more than 7 million users with presence in over 100 countries.

Paktor Labs projects

Down

Down, the #1 casual dating app, and Sweet, the romantic version, were founded by Colin Hodge in 2013.

Down, Inc. was acquired by the Paktor Group in 2016, with Founder & CEO Colin Hodge joining the company as VP of Labs. On a combined basis, these two apps account for more than 5 million organically-acquired users within the Paktor Group, with healthy profits and strong annual revenues.

Down and Sweet are particularly popular in North America, Brazil, Germany, and the UK.

Kickoff

Kickoff, a friend-of-friends dating app, was founded in 2014 and is hugely popular in Brazil and various other Latin American countries.

Acquired in 2016, Kickoff adds a strong presence in Latin America for the Paktor Group.

Goodnight

Goodnight was founded by Howard Yang and Andy Huang in August 2015.

Goodnight is a voice-based friend and relationship discovery app, where you can say “goodnight” with other users nearby. A top-10 social app in Taiwan with around 1m users, Goodnight focuses on voice, rather than photos or chat, as a means to make friends by talking with nearby Stars, talking randomly with nearby users, or posting voice notes in the thriving forums.

Since joining the Paktor Group, Goodnight has experienced over 1,000% growth.
