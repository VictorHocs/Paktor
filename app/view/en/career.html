5 Paktor Company Values

01. Over Communicate

You can’t read other people’s minds and no one can read yours.

The only way to be on the same page is to talk. If you have a question, ask. If you’re not sure, ask. If you want to know what the other person is doing, ask. If you think someone might be interested in your work, tell them. If you’re not sure if your work is relevant to what you are trying to achieve, share it.

When others share, you will learn and vice versa.

02. Act Global

The internet doesn’t have borders, we don’t either.

We work in a global team, respect the diversity amongst us. We’re not competing locally, we’re competing with the world. This means measuring ourselves against the best in the world.

We source from the world’s best talents, compete in markets worldwide and look for solutions from the world’s best practitioners.

03. Never Stop Growing

Constantly challenge and push yourself and your team.

This is a place for growth, we will support you. If you don’t fail once in awhile, you’re not trying hard enough. If work feels stale, you’ve stopped growing. There is always room to grow. Get better at what you do, learn what others are doing, help your teammates get better.

When you grow, the company will grow.

04. Be Data Driven

If you can measure it, do it. If you’re not sure, experiment.

By constantly testing, learning, and optimizing, the company becomes a living laboratory. If your opinion goes against data, change your opinion. Decisions are not feelings, dice or astrology. Hope is not a strategy, luck is not a factor. If you can’t measure and there is no data available, take the plunge.

For everything else, use the available data.

05. It’s Day One

Live every day like it’s Day One of the company.

The future is up to us to build, nothing is set in stone. Current ways of thinking and doing can and will change. Things can get dirty, not everything is nice and in its place, things may break.

The path ahead is long and we just started. There is still much to do.
